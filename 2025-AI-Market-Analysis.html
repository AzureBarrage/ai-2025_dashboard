The Rise of Multipolarity

Insight: Market Erosion

Sovereign AI Initiatives

The chart indicates that while North America (63.5%) maintains a "Super Brain" monopoly, the APAC region is expanding at a CAGR of 19.8%.

This shift is driven by Sovereign AI initiatives—nations building their own infrastructure to reduce reliance on US-based cloud providers. By 2025, 65% of governments worldwide have introduced sovereignty requirements for AI systems.

Conclusion: We are transitioning from a centralized AI era to a distributed, regionalized ecosystem.

Economic Impact & Market Volatility

The "Efficiency Paradox" on Wall Street

In early 2025, the "DeepSeek Shock" caused a 17% dip in Nvidia's share price as markets feared that high-cost GPU scaling was no longer the only path to intelligence.

However, by Q4, the narrative shifted. While per-token costs dropped by 90%, compute volume tripled as agentic AI began running autonomous background tasks 24/7. This has sustained massive demand for infrastructure despite unit-price deflation.

Technical Glossary

Sovereign AI: AI systems that a nation or enterprise fully controls, localized in domestic infrastructure to ensure data independence and security.
Agentic AI: Systems capable of taking autonomous actions to complete multi-step goals without continuous human prompting or intervention.
MoE (Mixture-of-Experts): An architectural strategy that activates only specialized subsets of a model's neurons per query, drastically reducing inference costs.
